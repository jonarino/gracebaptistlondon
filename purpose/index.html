Purpose Statement

The purpose of Grace Baptist Church is to develop believers in their relationship to the Lord Jesus Christ.

To that end, we are committed to honor the Lord through the proclamation of Biblical truth as the standard for obedience, the Christian life, Biblical worship (God-focused, not man-pleasing; Christ-centered, not experience-oriented; Holy Spirit-empowered, not emotionally driven), and service to others.

We seek to establish an environment and ministry which will produce individuals who will be an encouragement to fellow believers and be used to bring the unsaved to the Lord Jesus for salvation both at home and abroad.
